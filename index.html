Generative AI has a testing problem.

Advancements are being made at breakneck speed. Indeed, every week seems to be a year when it comes to the proliferation of new models or the release of a new technical paradigm. However, there is no reliable method to ascertain the actual ability of these models over time. This is where LayerLens comes in.

Easy Evaluation for AI Models

Our goal is to provide businesses and developers with an easy, digestible way to assess AI model performance for industry-specific use-cases.

In-depth Analysis

We provide a wide set of metrics for every evaluation that we run, allowing for in-depth analysis into exactly how well a model is performing on a specific use-case.

Wide Coverage

We currently have the widest set of evaluation datasets and models, with over 50 evaluation datasets and 200 generative AI models to pick and choose from.

New Evaluations

We plan on creating and supporting new evals for practical, everyday tasks.

Private Evaluations

We will support the execution of custom datasets, against both public models and custom AI applications.
